University and college holidays in the UK
 
One FREE place for every 20 places booked
 
Whether you are travelling as a club, society or group of friends, or want to reconnect with university alumni, our four brand-new university and college holidays are perfect for groups. Saga was the first holiday company to offer stays based at universities and colleges in the UK, and as part of our 75th anniversary celebrations we have reintroduced them into our portfolio with a modern twist. Designed for people over 50, they offer something different – a new style of accommodation, unbeatable well-placed locations, peak summer season at great prices, and a sociable atmosphere. These holidays might be the breath of fresh air you need, a great alternative to the traditional hotel city break.
