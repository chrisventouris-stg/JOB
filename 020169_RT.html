A SUMMER CONCERT IN TUSCANY
 
WITH ANDREA BOCELLI
 
After a sell-out success last year, we are delighted to be returning to Tuscany this summer to see Andrea Bocelli perform in his hometown Lajatico, at the open-air Teatro del Silenzio (Theatre of Silence)
 
Our five-night holiday based in ancient Lucca not only incudes tickets to see the world-renowned tenor sing in this atmospheric setting, it includes a walking tour of Lucca’s cobbled old town, a visit with a guided tour to Florence and a trip to seaside Viareggio by train.
 
There are now more places available on this popular holiday, but they are strictly limited, so book today to avoid disappointment!

Andrea Bocelli
Andrea Bocelli has left a lasting mark on the world of classical and contemporary music. With his powerful and emotive voice, he captivates audiences globally, selling over 75 million records worldwide, including his top hit Time to Say Goodbye. Born in Tuscany, he rose to prominence in the 1990s and has since performed on major stages across the world, collaborating with leading orchestras and well-known artists from pop and opera alike. His open-air concerts in his hometown of Lajatico have become a celebrated annual event.
