Andrea Bocelli
Andrea Bocelli has left a lasting mark on the world of classical and contemporary music. With his powerful and emotive voice, he captivates audiences globally, selling over 75 million records worldwide, including his top hit Time to Say Goodbye. Born in Tuscany, he rose to prominence in the 1990s and has since performed on major stages across the world, collaborating with leading orchestras and well-known artists from pop and opera alike. His open-air concerts in his hometown of Lajatico have become a celebrated annual event.
